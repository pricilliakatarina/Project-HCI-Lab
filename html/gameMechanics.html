What Will a Heroes Really Require?
So many options: How can I stay in the struggle for a longer period? Which of my abilities will send my opponent to his knees? Does this pair of boots go with my Divine Rapier? Even the best warrior requires guidance; use 'Game Mechanics' and Heroes help you master the finer nuances of strategy. You can now explore in-depth overviews of all your question about heroes in the 'Game Mechanics' section. The 'Heroes' section will tell you what skills that the hero has.

Q: What are a Heroes?
A Heroes is a way for players to gain knowledge about a specific hero. Heroes provide all the skills that the heroes have and the abilities of your hero. Heroes also contain the use of heroes to defeat the enemy

Q: How do I use Heroes?

To use a Heroes, you must choose the hero that you wanted to choose, click the "Heroes". From here you can see the detail skills of hero you are playing.
