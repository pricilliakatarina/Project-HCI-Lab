KEEPERS OF THE LIGHT

PROVIDES INSTANT MANA TO ALLIES

Keeper of the Light is a helpful addition to any team. Manipulating the mana of both his allies and enemies, he effortlessly destroys lesser foes with waves of illumination, then enters his spirit form to gather his team to press the assault.

ILLUMINATE

Channels light energy, building power the longer it's channeled. Once released, a wave is sent forth that deals damage and gives vision in its path. The longer it is channeled, the more damage is dealt.

SOLAR BIND

Weakens an enemy's magical resistance, and applies a slow that increases in strength the more the target moves.

CHAKRA MAGIC

Restores mana to the target unit and reduces cooldowns of all abilities currently on cooldown by a fixed amount.

BLINDING LIGHT

A blinding light flashes over the targeted area, knocking back and blinding the units in the area, causing them to miss some attacks. Knockback is 400.0 range over 0.4 seconds,

SPIRIT FORM

Ezalor temporarily turns his body luminescent, gaining bonus movement speed, cast range, grants the Blinding Light ability, and allows Illuminate to be channeled by a separate spirit and heal allies during the day.
